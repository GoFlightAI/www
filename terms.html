All charter flights are operated by independent, certified operators. GoFlight is not an air carrier, broker, or operator.

3. Escrow & Payment Terms

GoFlight uses an escrow payment model to protect both customers and operators:

  • When a customer accepts a bid, payment is collected and held in escrow by our payment processor.
  • Funds are released to the operator only after service delivery is confirmed.
  • GoFlight collects a 3% platform fee on each transaction.
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe. By using GoFlight, you also agree to Stripe's terms of service.

4. Cancellation Framework

Cancellation terms are set by each operator and displayed at the time of booking. General framework:

  • Operators define their own cancellation windows and refund percentages.
  • The 3% GoFlight platform fee is non-refundable once a booking is confirmed.
  • Refunds of the operator portion are subject to the operator's stated cancellation policy.
  • Disputes about cancellations should be raised through the platform's dispute resolution process.

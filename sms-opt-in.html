SMS / Text Messaging Opt-In

This page documents how end users consent to receive SMS text messages from GoFlight, what messages we send, and how to opt out. It is provided as the public opt-in record for our messaging program.

Program: GoFlight  ·  Effective Date: June 12, 2026

How end users opt in

End users opt in to SMS during account registration in the GoFlight web application, and can also opt in later from their profile and notification settings. The opt-in is an affirmative, unchecked checkbox shown directly beneath the phone-number field on the sign-up form.

Consent is voluntary and is never a condition of service. The phone number is optional, the SMS opt-in checkbox is unchecked by default, and it is separate from the (required) Terms of Service / Privacy Policy acceptance. A user can complete registration without opting in to SMS.

The sign-up form distinguishes two things directly at the point of phone entry:

  • A plain security notice stating that account-security texts (login codes, verification) are sent to help secure the account, with a message & data rates disclosure.
  • A separate, unchecked opt-in checkbox the user must affirmatively tick to receive non-security service notifications — stating message type, that frequency varies, that message & data rates may apply, and the STOP / HELP instructions, with the Privacy Policy and Terms linked inline.

Exact disclosure language

The following text is displayed verbatim on the sign-up form:

Security notice (shown to all) GoFlight will text you security messages — login codes and account verification — to help keep your account secure. Msg & data rates may apply.
SMS opt-in checkbox (affirmative, unchecked) Also text me GoFlight updates about trips, bookings, and platform activity. Message frequency varies; msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help anytime. See our Privacy Policy and Terms.

What messages we send

GoFlight sends transactional messages only — no marketing or promotional content. Two categories:

  • Account security (sent when a phone number is on file): multi-factor authentication login codes and account verification.
  • Service notifications (sent only to users who tick the opt-in checkbox): trip coordination, quote notifications, vendor/operator status changes, onboarding, and operational alerts related to active charter activity.

Representative sample messages

  • GoFlight: New quote request received for [route] on [MM/DD/YYYY]. View and respond at goflight.ai. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg&data rates may apply.
  • GoFlight: Trip [trip ID] update — [vendor] confirmed for [MM/DD/YYYY]. All parties notified. View status at goflight.ai. Reply STOP to opt out.
  • GoFlight: Your verification code is [code]. It expires in 10 minutes. Do not share this code.

How to opt out or get help

  • Opt out: reply STOP (also CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE) to any message at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts. You may also remove your phone number or disable SMS in your account settings.
  • Help: reply HELP for assistance, email support@goflight.ai, or visit goflight.ai.
  • Rates: message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.
Your mobile information is never shared or sold. Mobile information and SMS consent are not shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy.
